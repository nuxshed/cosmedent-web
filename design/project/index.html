Why Cosmedent

Four small things,
done consistently.

We don't promise miracles — we promise the basics, every visit, without compromise. It is these unglamorous standards that quietly shape a great clinical experience.

i

Honest diagnosis

Imaging-led examinations and second-opinion friendliness — we'll tell you when you don't need treatment.

ii

Sterile by default

Single-use disposables, autoclaved instruments and ISO-aligned protocols on every chair, every patient.

iii

Same doctors, every visit

No rotating residents. Continuity of care from consultation through to follow-up and maintenance.

iv

Transparent pricing

Itemised treatment plans shared up front, with cashless insurance and EMI options where applicable.

Your Visit

Calm, considered,
carefully paced.

From your first call to your last follow-up, every step is structured to remove the small anxieties that usually accompany a visit to the dentist.

Step 01

Reach out

A short call or message to understand what's bothering you and book a slot that suits.

Step 02

Examine

Digital X-rays, intra-oral photographs and a 30-minute conversation — never rushed.

Step 03

Plan

An itemised treatment plan with options, timelines and pricing emailed to you the same day.

Step 04

Treat & review

Treatment in calm, paced sittings with structured follow-ups — and a quiet aftercare plan.
